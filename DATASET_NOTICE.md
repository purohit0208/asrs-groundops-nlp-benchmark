# Dataset Notice

Created: 2026-06-04

This repository provides sanitized labels, split assignments, code, and result files for research reproducibility. It does not redistribute raw NASA ASRS narrative text.

NASA ASRS records remain the source data. Users are responsible for obtaining ASRS data through official ASRS channels and respecting ASRS caveats, de-identification boundaries, and use conditions.

The included labels and splits are intended for research benchmarking. They should not be used to infer operational prevalence, airline performance, safety rates, or real-world turnaround optimization effects.

Label provenance: 5,000 seed labels are author verified with local consistency-audit support. The 3,800 additional records are train-only agent-silver augmentation rows and are not newly human verified.
